Why Web Development?

Taking my work history off face value the first few questions you will be asking yourself is why web development? why now? The entirety of my working life has been spent in one industry, with one employer and pursuing one career, what would motivate someone who has worked up through a company to change lanes and learn something completely new? The answer however is quite simple.

Coding in general is always something I have found interesting along with the ever advancing technological world we live in. A step into this world is massively out of my comfort zone but i thrive on challenges and learning something new, all that i have learnt from my past work experience is vital in me learning a new trade. A solid work ethic, a knack for problem solving, a confident and approachable personality and the ability to excel as part of a team or when working solo. All cross over in what I believe are priceless assets.

The world of programming and development is a chance to create and solve issues which is a huge draw for me, it's a chance to branch out and develop new skills and in a new industry which will hopefully be as successful as the last. I believe the continued pursuit to develop and better yourself is never a bad move!

"Your work is going to fill a large part of your life, and the only way to be truly satisfied is to do what you believe is great work. And the only way to do great work is to love what you do." - Steve Jobs
